ABOUT ME

Think of the perfect son, the perfect brother, the perfect student.... all in all the perfect human being. Now, scratch all of that, because that’s not me, and I don’t think it’s possible to be perfect. I am as imperfect as any human can be. But there’s one thing I can promise: every day that I live, I strive to become better and move closer to that ideal. This blog is my way of sharing my thoughts and experiences along this journey of growth and self-improvement.
